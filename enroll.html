Frequently Asked Questions

How do I secure a spot for my child at Prodeo Academy?

The first step is to fill out an application for admission through School Mint: https://prodeoacademy.schoolmint.net/. In March, we will hold a lottery and offer admissions to families who are selected through the lottery.

Are Prodeo Academy schools good?

Prodeo Academy was featured on the 2018 High-Quality Charter Schools list by the Minnesota Department of Education. Over the last five years, Prodeo Academy students have shown tremendous academic results. Read more about our history and commitment to academic excellence here: https://prodeoacademy.org/about/history.

Are Prodeo Academy schools private?

No. Prodeo Academy schools are tuition-free, open-enrollment charter schools that provide a college preparatory education.

Which grades do Prodeo Academy schools serve?

In the 2019-20 school year, Prodeo’s Minneapolis campus will be offering Pre-Kindergarten through 6th grade, and Prodeo’s St. Paul campus will be offering Pre-Kindergarten through 2nd grade and will also offer 6th grade.
