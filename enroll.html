Why Prodeo?

Prodeo Academy was featured on the 2018 High-Quality Charter Schools list by the Minnesota Department of Education. Over the last five years, Prodeo Academy students have shown tremendous academic results. Read more about our history and commitment to academic excellence here: https://prodeoacademy.org/about/history.

At Prodeo Academy, your child will receive:

  • Personalized learning in small classes of 23-25 students
  • Yoga, mindfulness, and fitness instruction
  • Free transportation
  • Free breakfast and lunch

Frequently Asked Questions:

Does Prodeo cost money?

No. Prodeo Academy schools are tuition-free, open-enrollment charter schools that provide a college preparatory education. Which grades do Prodeo Academy schools serve?

In the 2019-20 school year, Prodeo’s Minneapolis campus will be offering Pre-Kindergarten through 6th grade, and Prodeo’s St. Paul campus will be offering Pre-Kindergarten through 2nd grade.
