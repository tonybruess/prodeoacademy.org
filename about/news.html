Prodeo Academy to Expand at New Saint Paul Campus January 30, 2018

On January 12th, Prodeo Academy’s affidavit was awarded by the Minnesota Department of Education, allowing Prodeo Academy to replicate its proven, high-quality academic model at a new campus in Saint Paul.

"On behalf of the Prodeo Academy Board of Directors, we are pleased our authorizer, Innovative Quality Schools, and the Minnesota Department of Education, approved our expansion into St. Paul," says Executive Director Rick Campion. "We’re excited to be able to provide families in St. Paul with a high-quality school option for their children."

The new school’s campus will be the educational facilities located at St. Bernard’s Catholic Church, 170 Rose Avenue West, St. Paul, MN 55117. During the first year the school will begin with pre-kindergarten through first grade, adding a new class each year until eighth grade, and growing to serve over 800 students. The new campus has enough educational space to accommodate the school at full enrollment, along with exciting auxiliary facilities, including a large cafeteria and two gymnasiums.
